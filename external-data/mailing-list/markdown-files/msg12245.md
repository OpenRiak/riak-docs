All you .NET folks, you'll want to update your CorrugatedIron libraries.

CorrugatedIron v1.4.2 fixes two significant bugs

\* Fixed (#171) where protobuf messages weren't making it through
`GetObject` due to a MemoryStream not being reset correctly
\* In the process of implementing the protobuf fix, fixed one memory leak in
`GetObject` and another in `SetObject`
