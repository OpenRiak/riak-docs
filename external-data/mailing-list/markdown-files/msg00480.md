> 2) Q --- Does riak replicate the bucket information (e.g. list of keys
> inside the bucket)? (from ddmmi via #riak
>
>    A --- It's not replicated, it's gossiped along with ring state.
>
> "Gossiping" in the Riak Glossary --->
> http://wiki.basho.com/display/RIAK/Riak+Glossary#RiakGlossary-Gossiping

Just reread the Recap and wanted to articulate something so as not to
confuse anyone...

The bucket properties are gossiped around the cluster. The list of
keys in a bucket is not gossiped but rather generated across the
cluster on-demand.
