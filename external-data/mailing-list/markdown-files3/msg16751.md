Hi Ricardo,

You want to put it it the top-level of your app.config file, in the bitcask
section:

 {bitcask,
 [{io\_mode,erlang},
 {merge\_window,...},
 ...,
 ...
 ]},


http://docs.basho.com/riak/1.4.9/ops/advanced/backends/multi/
