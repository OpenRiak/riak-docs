Hi,

If you do not have siblings enabled and are certain ALL values are small, there 
are a few other things that can cause performance problems:

- Are you running key or bucket listings?
- Are you running secondary index queries or mapreduce jobs?
- Can you confirm you have disabled swap?
- Are you monitoring riak statistics? Do you have a graph of the latencies you 
can share? 

Also, can you provide the complete output from statistics when the slowdown 
happens for us to look at?

Best regards,
