&gt; Hi Neville,

&gt; Riak Search uses the JVM to run analyzers. Analyzers are configured using
&gt; the analyzer\_factory option in the schema (set schema wide or per field).
&gt; Analyzers determine how incoming data is broken down and indexed, for
&gt; example a white space analyzer would break a document into words by
&gt; splitting on white space characters and indexing the words.
&gt;
&gt; Regarding the schema, this section of the wiki should answer your question:
