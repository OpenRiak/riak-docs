Brisa,

You want to use the 'wt=json' parameter, in your query (see the Solr
section of http://docs.basho.com/riak/latest/dev/using/search/ )
So, your request would be:
http://127.0.0.1:10018/solr/index\_bucket/select?q=key:Value&wt=json

(As a side note, in HTTP terminology, the 'Content-Type: application/json'
header describes the format of your \_request\_, not the response).
