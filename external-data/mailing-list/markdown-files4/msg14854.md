&gt; &gt; &gt; the following to change the logging level, to debug, then run

&gt; &gt; &gt;
&gt; &gt; &gt; - `riak attach`
&gt; &gt; &gt; - `(riak@nodename)1&gt; SetDebug = fun() -&gt; {node(), 
&gt; &gt; &gt; lager:set\_loglevel(lager\_file\_backend, "/var/log/riak/console.log", 
&gt; &gt; &gt; debug)} end.`
&gt; &gt; &gt; - `(riak@nodename)2&gt; rp(rpc:multicall(erlang, apply, [SetDebug,[]])).`
&gt; &gt; &gt; (don't forget the period at the end of these statements)
&gt; &gt; &gt; - Hit CTRL+C twice to quit from the node
&gt; &gt; &gt;
&gt; &gt; &gt; You can then revert back to the normal `info` logging level by running 
&gt; &gt; &gt; the following command via `riak attach`:
&gt; &gt; &gt;
&gt; &gt; &gt; - `riak attach`
&gt; &gt; &gt; - `(riak@nodename)1&gt; SetInfo = fun() -&gt; {node(),

&gt; &gt; &gt; info)} end.`

&gt; &gt; &gt; - Hit CTRL+C twice to quit from a the node
