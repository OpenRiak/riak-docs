&gt; Hi Ildar,
&gt; 
&gt; Please have a look at the configuration files: /etc/riak/app.config and 
&gt; /etc/riak/vm.config
&gt; 
&gt; By default Riak binds to localhost, but you can change that using the 
&gt; following snippet:
&gt; 
&gt; export riakIP=$(ifconfig eth0 | grep 'inet addr' | cut -d: -f2 | cut -d' ' 
&gt; -f1)
&gt; 
&gt; sudo sed -i "s/127.0.0.1/$riakIP/ " 
&gt; /etc/riak/app.config /etc/riak/vm.args
&gt;
