&gt; Riak S2 2.1 (aka Riak CS) is available. Thanks to all who made it happen!
&gt;
&gt; Here are the highlights of the release:
&gt; \* New metrics that enable you to determine the health of your Riak S2
&gt; system, as well as get reports on your storage utilization per bucket
&gt; or user.
&gt; \* Garbage collection refinements
&gt; \* A module-level hook point for limiting user access and quota usage
&gt; \* General availability of multi-bag, now known as supercluster
