&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; If you have objects that can be a s big as 15MB, it is probably wise to 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; increase the size of +zdbbl in order to avoid filling up buffers when 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; these large objects need to be transferred between nodes. What an 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; appropriate level is depends a lot on the size distribution of your 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; data and your access patterns, so I would recommend benchmarking to 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; find a suitable value.

&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; which may be what you are hitting. You can override this to 256k by 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; adding the following line to the vm.args file:
