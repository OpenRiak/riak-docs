On Tue, Jul 23, 2013 at 6:51 AM, rsb  wrote:
&gt; Is there any underlaying difference between performing an;
&gt;
&gt; storeObject.withoutFetch().execute();
&gt; -or-
&gt; myBucket.store(item.key, item).execute();
&gt;
&gt; In other words, will my second statement result in an implicit fetch as
&gt; well?

Bucket.store() returns a StoreObject. You want to call its
withoutFetch() method:

myBucket.store(item.key, item).withoutFetch().execute();
