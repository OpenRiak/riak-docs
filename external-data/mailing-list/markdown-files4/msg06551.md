Riak Recap for February 3 - 5
======================

1) Basho Hacker Sean Cribbs will be talking about Riak at the "NoSQL in The
Real World" event that Gilt Group is putting on Wednesday night in New York
City.

\* Event details here ---&gt; http://nosql-tech-talk.eventbrite.com/

2) Buddy Lindsey wrote up his thoughts on Riak based on the Riak Chapter
from the forthcoming "7 Databases in 7 Weeks" book.

\* Read here ---&gt;
http://buddylindsey.com/riak-week-2-of-7-databases-in-7-weeks

3) Ilya Grigorik wrote an extensive piece on LevelDB.

\* Read here ---&gt;
http://www.igvita.com/2012/02/06/sstable-and-log-structured-storage-leveldb/

4) Basho Hacker Russell Brown will be doing a Riak tutorial in May at the
NoSQL event that SkillsMatter is putting on.

\* Details and Registration here ---&gt;
http://skillsmatter.com/event/nosql/progressive-nosql-tutorials/ac-3538

Remixation, talking about Mecha, a search and analytics system for Riak
that they use in production.

\* Details here --&gt;
http://www.meetup.com/San-Francisco-Riak-Meetup/events/51287272/
