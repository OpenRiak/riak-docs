&gt; Assuming you're using Riak 2.0.x, you can simply comment out any lines in
&gt; riak.conf that begin "listener.http.". For example, the default config says
&gt; this:
&gt;
&gt; listener.http.internal = 127.0.0.1:8098
&gt;
&gt; Change it to this:
&gt;
&gt; # listener.http.internal = 127.0.0.1:8098
&gt;
&gt; You could also just delete the line from riak.conf.
