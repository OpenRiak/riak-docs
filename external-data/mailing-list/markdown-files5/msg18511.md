&gt; On 2 Mar 2018, at 09:44, Russell Brown  wrote:
&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Hi,
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Just an update on the progress of riak-2.2.5 release. I realize we said
&gt;&gt; "end of 2017" and then "end of Jan" and then "end of Feb" and here we
&gt;&gt; are, 1st March, spring is upon us, and still no 2.2.5. I thought it best
&gt;&gt; to at least keep you posted.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Why no release? Well, we're not quite finished yet. In terms of what is
&gt;&gt; left:
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; - a few PRs need review and merge against the upstream Basho repo (see
&gt;&gt; [1] if you want to help there);
&gt;&gt; - Opening of PRs for gsets;
&gt;&gt; - Docs for the changes;
&gt;&gt; - Release notes;
&gt;&gt; - Tagging;
&gt;&gt; - A final round of testing (and fixing?) after all is merged;
&gt;&gt; - Client support. This is crucial, but all the above work only has
&gt;&gt; client support in the Basho erlang clients. I'm hoping the community
&gt;&gt; that uses the Java/Python/Ruby etc clients can step up here. But we can 
&gt;&gt; release Riak before all client work is done.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; The optimist in me says 2 weeks.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; What do I mean "release"?
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; From my point of view the release is the tags. After that I'm sincerely
&gt;&gt; hoping ESL will continue to kindly build and host the actual artifacts.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; What's in the release?
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; As a reminder, since it's been a while since StokeCon17, this release
&gt;&gt; contains:
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; - Some developer clean-up around `make test` and `riak\\_test` to make
&gt;&gt; them more reliable/trustworthy;
&gt;&gt; - Open source MDC Repl (thanks bet365!);
&gt;&gt; - A fix for a bug in riak core claim that led to unbalanced rings[2];
&gt;&gt; - `node\\_confirms` a feature like `w` or `pw` but for physical diversity in 
&gt;&gt; durability[3];
&gt;&gt; - `participate in coverage` an admin setting that takes a node out of
&gt;&gt; the coverage plan (for example after adding a node while transfers
&gt;&gt; take place);
&gt;&gt; - Riak repl fix, and change to unsafe default behaviour[4];
&gt;&gt; - Addition of a GSet to riak data types;
&gt;&gt; - Fix to repl stats[5].
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Sorry if I missed anything. The release notes will have it all.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Work is already begun on Riak 3.0 with OTP20 support well under way.
&gt;&gt; Some candidates for inclusion that we're working on are a new
&gt;&gt; pure-Erlang backend[6], and a radical overhaul of AAE for both intra and 
&gt;&gt; inter-cluster anti-entropy.
&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt; Sorry for the delay, thanks for your patience. We’ll keep you posted.
&gt;&gt;
