On Sat, Jun 9, 2012 at 12:55 AM, Zabrane Mickael  wrote:

&gt; Hi Trevor,
&gt;
&gt; We've teh latest Riak up and running on Fedora 17 with R15B01.
&gt;
&gt; Follow these are the steps:
&gt;
&gt; $ wget
&gt; http://downloads.basho.com.s3-website-us-east-1.amazonaws.com/riak/CURRENT/riak-1.1.2.tar.gz
&gt; $ tar xzvf riak-1.1.2.tar.gz
&gt; $ cd riak-1.1.2
&gt; $ ./rebar get-deps
&gt;
&gt; Remove this line "{require\\_otp\\_vsn, "R14B0[234]|R15"}." from all these
&gt; files:
&gt; $ find . -name "rebar.config" | xargs grep "require\\_otp\\_vsn"
&gt;
&gt; ./deps/cluster\\_info/rebar.config:{require\\_otp\\_vsn, "R13B04|R14"}.
&gt; ./deps/riak\\_kv/rebar.config:{require\\_otp\\_vsn, "R13B04|R14"}.
&gt; ./deps/riak\\_search/rebar.config:{require\\_otp\\_vsn, "R13B04|R14"}.
&gt; ./rebar.config:{require\\_otp\\_vsn, "R14B0[234]"}.
&gt;
&gt; Build a RIAK release:
&gt; $ yum -y groupinstall "Development Tools"
&gt; $ make rel
&gt;
&gt; When done:
&gt; $ su
&gt; # mv rel/riak /opt/riak
&gt;
&gt; Then, try it:
&gt; cd /opt/riak
&gt; ./bin/riak console
&gt;
&gt; Good luck.
&gt;
&gt; Regards,
&gt; Zabrane
