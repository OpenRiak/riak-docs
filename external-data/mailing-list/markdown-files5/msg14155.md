I plan to add Riak support to Disco. Disco has an abstract concept of
Worker (currently
python, ocaml, java are available and golang and haskell are on
their way). Writing a new worker should be pretty simple (e.g. take a look
at the golang worker in progress: github.com/discoproject/goworker).

Ideally, we would like to combine disco nodes and riak nodes (something
like https://github.com/pavlobaron/disco\\_playground which will be the base
of my work).

Regards.
