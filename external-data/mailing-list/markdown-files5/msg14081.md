&gt; 
&gt; Hi Paul,
&gt; 
&gt; Deleting a key in Riak will result it being removed from the Yokozuna/Solr 
&gt; index.
&gt; 
&gt; Call the delete method against a bucket object and pass in the name of the 
&gt; key as an argument, such as: 
&gt; 
&gt; pauls\\_bucket = client.bucket('foo')
&gt; pauls\\_bucket.delete('bar')
&gt; 
&gt; You can also call delete against a riak object if you've previously fetched 
&gt; it. 
&gt;
