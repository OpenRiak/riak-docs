&gt;
&gt; HI Taufan,
&gt;
&gt; riakc\\_pb\\_socket connections are usually long lived, and will stay open
&gt; until disconnected or timed out while performing a specific task. There is
&gt; no specific code in the riak\\_pb\\_server for ensuring that idle connections
&gt; will be closed after a given period of inactivity.
&gt;
&gt; If you’re opening and closing riakc\\_pb\\_socket connections at various
&gt; places in your application — maybe consider using a connection pool for
&gt; maintaining a known set of open connections that you use for issuing
&gt; requests?
