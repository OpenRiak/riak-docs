&gt; Is there documentation to indicate what riak search supports in search

The best available documentation is here:
http://wiki.basho.com/Riak-Search.html
 
&gt; 
&gt; I have been testing some searches and even looked at:
&gt; 
&gt; http://lucene.apache.org/core/old\\_versioned\\_docs/versions/3\\_5\\_0/queryparsersyntax.html
&gt; 
&gt; for some ideas.
&gt; 
&gt; These work:
&gt; 
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:Water"&gt;&gt;).
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:water"&gt;&gt;).
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:water OR notes:Water"&gt;&gt;).
&gt; 
&gt; But these don't
&gt; 
&gt; ? seems to work at the end but not in the middle. Same with \\*. Why?
&gt; 
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:Wate?"&gt;&gt;) works.
&gt; 
&gt; But not
&gt; 
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:Wa?er"&gt;&gt;).

Currently wildcard searches are only supported at the end of the term:
http://wiki.basho.com/Riak-Search---Querying.html#Wildcard-Searches
 
&gt; 
&gt; 
&gt; How would you search for "&"? maybe it is too short to be searched on
&gt; 
&gt; search:search\\_doc(&lt;&lt;"searchable-dbooks"&gt;&gt;,&lt;&lt;"notes:&"&gt;&gt;). In order to
&gt; index "&", I had to use "&":
&gt; 
&gt; Category: Water & Wetlands (W).

I was able to search for "&" by escaping the character:
search-cmd search wildcards "value:\\&"
