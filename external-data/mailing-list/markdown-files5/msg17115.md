&gt;&gt; Since you are running with allow\\_mult=false, make sure the clocks of your
&gt;&gt; Riak nodes are synchronized. If they are out of sync, newer values may get
&gt;&gt; overridden by older ones on read, depending on the node a request hits
&gt;&gt; first. Of course this won't cover 100% of cases because a perfect clock
&gt;&gt; synchronization is just impossible.
&gt;&gt;
&gt;&gt; Also, setting notfound\\_ok to "false" may help if you encounter not founds
&gt;&gt; for keys you are sure have been written.
