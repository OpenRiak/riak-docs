&gt; Hi Ray,

&gt; Deletion is replicated by both real-time and full-sync MDC -
&gt; specifically, the tombstone representing the deletion will be
&gt; replicated. The delete\\_mode setting affects how long a tombstone
&gt; remains "alive" for MDC to replicate it (3 seconds by default). If
&gt; only full-sync MDC is used, the delete\\_mode should be modified to
&gt; allow a long enough lifetime for a full-sync operation to replicate
&gt; the tombstone.

&gt; http://docs.basho.com/riak/latest/ops/advanced/configs/configuration-files/

&gt; http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users\\_lists.basho.com/2011-October/006048.html
