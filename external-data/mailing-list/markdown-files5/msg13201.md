On Mon, Dec 9, 2013 at 10:08 PM, Hector Castro  wrote:

&gt; Hi Gavin,
&gt;
&gt; How have you configured your `storage\\_schedule`? It's possible that
&gt; the job for calculating storage statistics hasn't occurred (or
&gt; finished) yet.
&gt;
&gt; You can manually trigger storage usage calculation by invoking the
&gt; following command:
&gt;
&gt; sudo riak-cs-storage batch
&gt;
&gt; From there, you can retrieve statistics from the `usage` bucket with
&gt; `s3cmd`:
&gt;
&gt; s3cmd get
&gt; s3://riak-cs/usage/.abj.20130501T000000Z.20130531T000000Z
&gt;
&gt; Also, here is a script I wrote up to access usage and storage
&gt; statistics with `s3cmd`:
&gt;
&gt; https://gist.github.com/hectcastro/126b5657f228096775c6
&gt;
&gt; --
&gt; Hector
