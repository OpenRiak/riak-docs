Riak Recap from May 7th to May 28th 2013.
====================================
 
1. Basho Hacker Ryan "LMAO" Zezeski added a Q & A Section to the Yokozuna
repo. Keep an eye on this for more updates.
- https://github.com/basho/yokozuna/blob/master/docs/Q-AND-A.md
 
2. Steve Vinoski was interviewed about Erlang.
- 
http://pdincau.wordpress.com/2013/05/14/an-interview-with-steve-vinoski-stev
evinoski/
 
- https://github.com/Weborama/Riak-Light
 
3. There's a new PHP Client from Kaspar Bach Pedersen
- https://github.com/TriKaspar/php\\_riak
 
4. Basho Hacker Eric Redmond will be leading a Riak and Erlang meetup while
he's in Israel this June.
- http://www.meetup.com/IGTCloud/events/118984892/


5. Techtalk on Riak & Riak CS at South Bay, California on May 30th. Location
TBD today.
- http://www.meetup.com/Silicon-Valley-NoSQL/events/120919612/


6. Will Laurance's Rust just hit 0.16
- https://github.com/wlaurance/rust
 
- https://pypi.python.org/pypi/riak\\_cli\\_tool/0.1.5
 
7. Basho TE Adron Hall begins a series taking a closer look at Riak (and
distributed systems in general) with a post on backup and recovery
- http://compositecode.com/2013/05/08/distributed-databases-issue-001/
 
8. Riak gets an extensive write-up in Scalatra guide. Scalatra is a "simple,
accessible and free web micro-framework."
- http://www.scalatra.org/2.2/guides/persistence/riak.html
 
9. Rovio has been rolling out services on Riak. A video of their talk from
GDC (delivered alongside Basho Chief Architect Andy Gross) is now published
on the Basho Blog.
- http://basho.com/gdc-video-riak-at-rovio/
 
10. Basho hacker Nathan Aschbacher just released a tool for demonstrating
Riak's durability characteristics.
- https://github.com/nathanaschbacher/riak-durability-demo
 
11. Basho hacker Eric Redmond is giving a talk called "Riak: Built
Distributed" at the upcoming DevCon in Tel Aviv, Israel this June.
- 
http://devcon-june2013.events.co.il/presentations/888-riak-built-distributed
 
12. Basho TE John Daily has followed up his recent behavioral configurations
blog series with an epilogue inspired by talks at RICON East.
- http://basho.com/riaks-config-behaviors-epilogue/
 
13. Jon Brisbin announced a Reactor library named Riaktor.
- 
http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users\\_lists.basho.com/2013-May/012112.
html
 
14. David Pollack is building a Splunk killer.
- http://blog.goodstuff.im/plugh
 
15. Polyglot Conf http://polyglotconf.com/
May 24th-26th in Vancouver BC, CA
 
16. Jeremey Ong wrote a post about why he's starting to develop against
Dynamo-style DBs. Riak gets a nice plug.
- http://www.jeremyong.com/blog/2013/05/11/on-choosing-dynamo/
 
17. The team at CopperEgg wrote up a short post about how to plug Riak in
their monitoring platform.
- http://copperegg.com/riak-monitoring-database-week/
 
18. Basho Coworking Office Hours in Portland
- http://www.meetup.com/Portland-Riak/events/115834892/
May 13th in Portland, OR
 
19. Node PDX http://nodepdx.org/
May 16 & 17th in Portland, Oregon
