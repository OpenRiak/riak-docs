Take a look at riak-banana[1]. We are able to make this work by pointing
banana queries to the SOLR endpoint instead of Riak due to some
incompatibilities with banana queries and our Riak API. As things change
this may be a non-issue going forward but as of now the easiest thing to do
it just point banana directly at Solr.
