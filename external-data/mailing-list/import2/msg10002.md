Riak Users,

We have an updated release candidate for Riak 1.3.0 (RC2).

It is available for download here:
http://docs.basho.com/riak/1.3.0rc2/downloads/

The items changed for RC2 are below and are reflected in the release notes:
https://github.com/basho/riak/blob/1.3/RELEASE-NOTES.md


 - Permissions not checked on $PIPE\_DIR in riak script:
 https://github.com/basho/riak/issues/192
 - convert LRUCache from LRU to simple fifo:
 https://github.com/basho/leveldb/pull/71
 - Take node liveness into account during contant hash choice for reduce
 phase: https://github.com/basho/riak\_kv/issues/476 - Thanks Gunin
 Alexander
 - Improve interaction between AAE and K/V deletion:
 https://github.com/basho/riak\_kv/pull/478


Thanks for the continued support,
-Basho
