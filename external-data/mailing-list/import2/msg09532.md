Hey David, 
You are correct. You can do 1 of two things here: 

1) Mount your 1TB volume to /var/lib/riak. All ring, and backend data will be 
stored there. 

2) Mount your volume somewhere like /data/riak and switch the platform\_data\_dir 
config option to reflect that mounted path. 

If you wanted to snapshot your riak data, it all lives in platform\_data\_dir


Hope this helps,
