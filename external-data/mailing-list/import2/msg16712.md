Hi Mohamad.

To keep your data available, Riak duplicates data and stores it on multiple
nodes. The default number of replicas for a stored value is 3. If you have
4TB of image data, you should expect it to take up 12TB of space (+ size of
metadata) in your Riak cluster.

What is the total amount of data stored in the bitcask directories of all
your nodes?
