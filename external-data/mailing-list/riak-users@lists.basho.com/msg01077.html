4)  Q --- Any word on future riak core posts from kevin smith? (from
technoweenie via #riak)

     A --- More posts are planned but, sadly, there is no specific

 For those of you who haven't had a chance to read it, here is the

5)  Q --- On the Links and Link Walking page it says Riak can
substitute for a graph DB "as long as the number of links is kept
reasonably low." What "number of links" does this refer to? The total
number of links in the whole Riak cluster? or the number of outgoing
links on any given document? (from pjstadig via #riak)

     A -- The number of links on any given document. (I've updated the
wiki to clarify that.)

6) j2043 kicked off a brief but valuable conversation with bingledac
and benblask when he asked "With Riak 0.12 Is there an upper limit on
the number of keys in a bucket? "

Gist here ---> http://gist.github.com/581141
