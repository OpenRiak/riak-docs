[ANN] Riak KV 2.0.7

Seema Jethani Thu, 30 Jun 2016 15:50:34 -0700 

Riak Users,

Riak 2.0.7 was released on June 27th and can be downloaded from the
downloads page[1]. This release contains new updates to batching and
Solr, Cuttlefish configurations, an OTP upgrade, and fixes for two
product advisories[2]. Please review the Release Notes [3] for more
details before upgrading.

[1] *http://docs.basho.com/riak/kv/2.0.7/downloads/
<http://docs.basho.com/riak/kv/2.0.7/downloads/>*

[2] http://docs.basho.com/riak/kv/2.0.7/release-notes/#product-advisories

[3] *http://docs.basho.com/riak/kv/2.0.7/release-notes/
<http://docs.basho.com/riak/kv/2.0.7/release-notes/>*
