Re: webcast on riak_core?

Jon Brisbin Mon, 04 Apr 2011 12:30:00 -0700 

I'm really interested to see how we can graduate from simple OTP to a real 
distributed app that parallelizes workloads, partitions data, the whole nine 
yards... :)

I know start-ups are using core already to power Erlang-based cloud apps 
(riak_core, Riak, maybe a little messaging thrown in for good measure :) and 
after getting into it, I can see why.

Anything geared toward cloud-scale apps would be great. What to put on Riak 
itself, what to put in your app w/ riak_core. How to handle netsplits, etc... I 
can think of loads of things from the super simple to the super complex.

jb
