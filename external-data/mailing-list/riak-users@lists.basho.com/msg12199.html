Riak Recap for August 28 - September 5
====================
Riak 1.4.2 was released.
- 
http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users_lists.basho.com/2013-August/013183.html

Basho announced a new development process for its repositories on GitHub.
- 
http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users_lists.basho.com/2013-August/013148.html

Dan & Drew Kerrigan of Basho discuss client-side CRDTs and term-based inverted 
indexes, including a demonstration Zombie Index application.
- http://basho.com/index-for-fun-and-for-profit/
- http://basho.com/indexing-the-zombie-apocalypse-with-riak/
- http://zombies.samples.basho.com/

Jonathan Ellis of Datastax wrote a blog post discussing the lack of vector 
clocks in Cassandra and talked extensively about Riak's default behaviors, 
resulting in a long and educational discussion on Hacker News.
- http://www.datastax.com/dev/blog/why-cassandra-doesnt-need-vector-clocks
- https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=6329619

Customer.io migrated from MongoDB to Riak and discussed the dramatic speed 
improvements they achieved.
- 
http://basho.com/customer-io-gains-6x-speed-improvement-by-moving-from-mongodb-to-riak/
- http://customer.io/blog/Segment-customer-data-faster.html

Flywheel, a mobile taxi hailing platform, selected Riak for its operational 
simplicity.
- 
http://basho.com/mobile-taxi-platform-flywheel-uses-riak-to-power-their-application/

Joachim Haagen Skeie open sourced a Riak data browser written in Ember.js and 
Netty.
- https://github.com/joachimhs/Embriak

John Vincent (Lusis) released an Omnibus project for Riak.
- https://github.com/lusis/omnibus-riak

Bryce Kerley asked for feedback on link walking via the Ruby library.
- 
http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users_lists.basho.com/2013-August/013187.html


Basho on the road
==
There are several opportunities to see Basho engineers at various meetups and 
conferences throughout September. See http://basho.com/events/ for a list.

Here are some events over the next couple of weeks:
* Surge in National Harbor, Maryland (http://surge.omniti.com/2013)
* PyCon APAC in Tokyo, Japan (http://apac-2013.pycon.jp/)
* Open Source Conference in Hokkaido, Japan (http://www.ospn.jp/osc2013-do/)
* Linux Cloud Open in New Orleans, Louisiana 
(http://events.linuxfoundation.org/events/cloudopen-north-america)
* NYC Riak Meetup featuring Gilt Group 
(http://www.meetup.com/NYC-Riak-Meetup/events/136926552/)
* Strange Loop in St. Louis, Missouri (https://thestrangeloop.com/)
* NoSQL Roadshow in Zurich, Switzerland 
(http://nosqlroadshow.com/nosql-zurich-2013/)
