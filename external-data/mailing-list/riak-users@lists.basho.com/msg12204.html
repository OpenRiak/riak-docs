This may not be a silver bullet for this issue but as far as version compatibility between Riak and Riak CS goes we have a chart here:

http://docs.basho.com/riakcs/latest/cookbooks/Version-Compatibility/

I'd highly recommend upgrading your Riak CS as there are some known issues with the 1.3.x series running against Riak 1.4.x
