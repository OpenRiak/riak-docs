On 2/8/14, 2:40 PM, Sean Cribbs wrote:

Hi Bryan,


You'll want to use a 1.4 branch/tag of riakc if you are connecting to 
Riak 1.4.x. The master branch is for Riak 2.0. Consider changing your 
dependency to be this:



{riakc, "1.4.*", {git, 
"git://github.com/basho/riak-erlang-client.git", {branch, "1.4"}}}


If that does not resolve your issue, please report back.
