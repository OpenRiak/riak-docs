Sean Cribbs Mon, 09 Jul 2012 04:31:48 -0700 

Hello Boris,

We do not advise using the strfun functionality in production, for obvious
security and stability reasons. That said, you can enable it on all of your
nodes by adding  this setting to the 'riak_kv' section of your app.config
file: {allow_strfun, true}

Cheers,
