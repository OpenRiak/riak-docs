Re: using links or list of ids

Guido Medina Thu, 07 Mar 2013 01:18:36 -0800 

This is only an idea:
You could mark your related objects with a 2i, when you need them retrieve the list of IDs from that 2i, fetch them concurrently into some local memory cache, and then navigate through your object graph by fetching them one by one from your local cache. It will be hard to find something that beats fetching the keys concurrently. 

Guido.
