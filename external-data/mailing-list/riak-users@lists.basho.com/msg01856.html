Re: problem with Riak search while searching with ruby client.

Rusty Klophaus Wed, 29 Dec 2010 17:40:02 -0800 

Hi Krishna,

Yes, you have encountered a known bug (
https://issues.basho.com/show_bug.cgi?id=864). To summarize, the Solr
interface has problems outputting date fields in JSON, but works just fine
in XML.

This is fixed in the upcoming 0.14 release.

Best,
Rusty
