Sean Cribbs Fri, 30 Aug 2013 08:14:02 -0700 

Riak does not parse the incoming data; whatever your client has sent to
Riak is what gets stored, verbatim. The client library is solely
responsible for (de-)serializing Ruby types into JSON.
