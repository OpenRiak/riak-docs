> We have a brief network maintenance(~15 min) coming up for 1 node in our 5
> node cluster.  I was wondering if best practice is to just let the network
> maintenance happen and let riak do the right thing or should we explicitly
> stop riak on that node during the maintenance and start it back up once the

completed (much like you would when upgrading a node).
