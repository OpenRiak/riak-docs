Julio,

You can't just give it the filename, you need to read the file.  Something
like this should work:

to_pdf(ReqData, Context) ->
  {ok, Body} = file:read_file("/root/sicap-rpt/reports/javascript.pdf"),
  {Body, ReqData, Context}.
