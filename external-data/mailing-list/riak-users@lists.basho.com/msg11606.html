Re: TCP recv timeout and handoffs almost all the time

Guido Medina Thu, 18 Jul 2013 11:37:27 -0700

If what you are describing is happening for 1.4, type riak-admin diag and see the new recommended kernel parameters, also, on vm.args uncomment the +zdbbl 32768 parameter, since what you are describing is similar to what happened to us when we upgraded to 1.4. 

HTH,

Guido.
