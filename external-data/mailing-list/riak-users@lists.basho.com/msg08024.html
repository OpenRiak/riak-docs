Jeremy,

Your index field must have a "_bin" or "_int" suffix on it to signify
whether it is a binary or an integer. Add "_bin" to the field name you have
in your example, e.g.

MetaData = dict:store(<<"index">>, [{<<"afield_bin">>, <<"avalue">>}],
dict:new())
