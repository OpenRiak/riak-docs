Riak Recap for November 23 - 27
========================

1) Version 0.0.2 of resourceful-riak is out.

* Code and details here --->
https://twitter.com/#!/nodenpm/status/141165957975523328

2) Basho CTO Justin Sheehy will be speaking about Riak at the Twin
Cities NoSQL Meetup next week in Richfield, MN.

* Details and registration here --->
http://twincitiesnosqlmeetup.eventbrite.com/

3) Jeremy Walworth released a Password Vault that uses Riak as the
backing store.

* Code here on GitHub --->
https://github.com/jeremyfsu/Riak-Backed-Password-Vault

4) Basho Team Members Steve Vinoski and Justin Sheehy collaborated
with Kresten Krab Thorup and Debasish Ghosh on a short paper called
"Programming language impact on the development of distributed
system".

* Definitely worth the read --->
http://www.springerlink.com/content/w071241962270242/fulltext.pdf

5) We are doing office hours this Thursday at BashoWest in San Francisco.

* Details here --->
http://www.meetup.com/San-Francisco-Riak-Meetup/events/42645002/

6) Q --- Are people running riak natively on osx (for development) or
running on a vm that matches production?  (from kenperkins via #riak)

    A --- Anyone? (We had a similar thread on the list several months
back about this but I figured it couldn't hurt to open it up to more
discussion.)
