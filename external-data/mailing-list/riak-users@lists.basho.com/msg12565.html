Re: Unable to configure Riak-CS-Control to manage users

Dmitri Zagidulin Wed, 09 Oct 2013 07:47:46 -0700 

Hi Siddhu,

Can you try changing 'cs_proxy_host' to localhost? So:

 {cs_proxy_host, "127.0.0.1" }.

and retry.

>  *N**ode-1*
>
