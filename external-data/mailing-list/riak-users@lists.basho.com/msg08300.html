Re: How to store a list in riak?

Kresten Krab Thorup Thu, 16 Aug 2012 06:01:33 -0700 

The most interesting piece of information is really how you expect to access 
the data.  That should be the starting point for designing the data layout.

Also, ... What are the kinds of lookups typically needed?  How many different 
"messages/" buckets do you expect? Order of magnitude numbers .... What kind of 
read/write performance do you expect?  How big are the messages?  Is the 
message/_meta object likely to be contended for writes?

Kresten
