Re: about random-generated keys and UUIDs

Alexander Sicular Fri, 11 Mar 2011 01:55:05 -0800 

Those random keys are not as random as you may think they are at first blush. 
If you look at the actual code, 
https://github.com/basho/riak_core/blob/master/src/riak_core_util.erl#L131, you 
will see that it is a base62 encoded sha hash of a unique reference within the 
erlang runtime, http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Erlang_Programming/Terms, and 
now(), aka. the time.

Cheers,
-Alexander Sicular
