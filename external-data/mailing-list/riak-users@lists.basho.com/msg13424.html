On 01/18/2014 02:16 AM, Charlie Voiselle wrote:
Invalid hint files will be rebuilt automatically during the Bitcask merge process. I believe you are seeing a one-time consequence of switching to signed hint files during the upgrade. Not to worry, they will be regenerated through normal merge activity. 

Regards,
