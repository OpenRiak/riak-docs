Riak Recap for May 1 - May 6
======================

John Daily has started a series of Basho blog posts on the subtleties
of Riak configuration.
- http://basho.com/understanding-riaks-configurable-behaviors-part-1/

John Liao wrote a blog post about Exploring Riak with F# and CorrugatedIron.
- 
http://jyliao.blogspot.com/2013/04/exploring-riak-with-f-and-corrugatediron.html

riak-js v0.10.1 is out.
- 
http://lists.basho.com/pipermail/riak-users_lists.basho.com/2013-May/012017.html
- http://riak-js.org/

Nathan LaFreniere wrote a blog post that details his journey through
the process of adding Protocol Buffers support to Node.js, and
eventually riak-js.
- http://blog.andyet.com/2013/may/7/riak-protocol-buffer-encoding-and-you/

Long-time Riak user Jason J. Williams (of digitar.com) ran the Leslie
Cohen Law 5k in LA recently. He sent the following photo along.
Congrats, Jason.
- http://photos.data.riakcs.net:8080/jjwilliams-riak.jpg

Q & A
- 
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/16191407/riak-1-3-1-will-not-start-on-lucid-ec2-instance
- 
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/16359656/riak-map-reduce-in-js-returning-limited-data
