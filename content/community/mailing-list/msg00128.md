&gt;&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt;&gt; 1) "heart" is probably not the best way to keep your Riak nodes up - it has 
&gt;&gt;&gt; a tendency to restart nodes that fail fatally, and doesn't behave well when 
&gt;&gt;&gt; you want to shut down a node normally. You might be better off with a 
&gt;&gt;&gt; traditional monitoring tool like "monit" which can back off restarts and 
&gt;&gt;&gt; notify you when a node is flapping.

&gt;&gt;&gt; 2) Make sure your configuration, either via editing vm.args or via the 
&gt;&gt;&gt; ERL\\_FLAGS environment variable, includes "-smp enable". Innostore won't 
&gt;&gt;&gt; work without it.
