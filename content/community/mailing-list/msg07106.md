On Fri, Mar 30, 2012 at 16:19, Jon Meredith  wrote:

&gt; Hi list,
&gt;
&gt; I spend some time looking at this issue today. I suspect it is due to all
&gt; the Javascript VMs being busy inside Riak due to many parallel MapReduce
&gt; jobs.
&gt;
&gt; If you're seeing this issue please check for this message in the
&gt; console.log file
&gt;
&gt; 16:40:50.037 [notice] JS call failed: All VMs are busy.
&gt;
&gt; I've filed https://github.com/basho/riak\\_pipe/issues/44 to track
&gt; improvements to error reporting.
&gt;
&gt; If you are seeing the log message, the number of VMs allocated for map and
&gt; reduce tasks can be configured in the app.config - defaults are 8 for map
&gt; and 6 for reduce, increasing them will use more memory but should reduce
&gt; the incidence of the error message.
&gt;
&gt; {riak\\_kv, [
&gt; ...
&gt; {map\\_js\\_vm\\_count, 24 },
&gt; {reduce\\_js\\_vm\\_count, 18 },
&gt; ...]
&gt;
&gt; If you were affected by it and changing this does not resolve your issue,
&gt; I'll keep digging.
&gt;
&gt; Cheers, Jon.
