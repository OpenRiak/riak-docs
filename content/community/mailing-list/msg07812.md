&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt; The major difference between these two is the first is executing a binary, 
&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt; which has to basically create everything (connection, payload, etc) every

&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt; something like your python script that will work on many threads/processes
