&gt; &gt;&gt; Sorry for the delay. At the moment, all metadata needs to be stored
&gt; &gt;&gt; under the &lt;&lt;"X-Riak-Meta"&gt;&gt; dict key. The following example
&gt; &gt;&gt; illustrates how this works:
&gt; &gt;&gt;
&gt; &gt;&gt; %% To store metadata:
&gt; &gt;&gt; Object = riakc\\_obj:new(&lt;&lt;"groceries"&gt;&gt;, &lt;&lt;"mine"&gt;&gt;, &lt;&lt;"eggs & bacon"&gt;&gt;).
&gt; &gt;&gt; MetaData = dict:from\\_list([{&lt;&lt;"X-Riak-Meta"&gt;&gt;, [{"Foo", "Bar"}]}]),
&gt; &gt;&gt; Object2 = riakc\\_obj:update\\_metadata(Object, MetaData).
&gt; &gt;&gt; riakc\\_pb\\_socket:put(Pid, Object2).
&gt; &gt;&gt;
&gt; &gt;&gt; %% To retrieve metadata:
&gt; &gt;&gt; {ok, O} = riakc\\_pb\\_socket:get(Pid, &lt;&lt;"groceries"&gt;&gt;, &lt;&lt;"mine"&gt;&gt;).
&gt; &gt;&gt; {ok, MD} = dict:find(&lt;&lt;"X-Riak-Meta"&gt;&gt;, riakc\\_obj:get\\_metadata(O)).
&gt; &gt;&gt;
&gt; &gt;&gt; We're kicking around ways to improve this in the future.
