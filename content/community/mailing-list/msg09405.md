&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; available if you explicitly select them in your gemfile using the 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; following:

&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt;

&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; The bundler/rubygems API won't negotiate a pre-release gem if you 
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; don't explicitly specify it in your gemfile.
&gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt; &gt;
