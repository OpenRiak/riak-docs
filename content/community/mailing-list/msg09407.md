&gt; Venki,

&gt; Regarding your original question about size of individual buckets/keys
&gt; within that data directory -- that cannot be done without directly
&gt; manipulating the storage engine or other components of Riak. One of the
&gt; benefits of Riak's consistent hashing is that you get a really even spread
&gt; among nodes and you don't really know (without digging into the Erlang
&gt; console) where specific keys live. That said, we have done it for customers
&gt; who have had specific production issues related to large keys, but this is
&gt; not something we recommend doing in general.
