Map/reduce is known to be slower than a standard fetch, so what you're
experiencing is likely a direct result of m/r overhead.

I would be interested in seeing if your test case shows a notable speed
difference against the current official release of Riak (0.14.2 I believe).
Based on what I've read of the in-development changes, performance should be
worse in the 0.14.2 release (assuming that the new m/r functionality based
on Riak Pipe is merged into whichever branch you're using). I may be worth
testing just to make sure you're not seeing worse performance (which could
suggest a new bug has been introduced).
